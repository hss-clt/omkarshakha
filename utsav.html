Makar Sankranti HSS (Hindu Swayamsevak Sangh) celebrates Makar Sankranti as a festival of new beginnings, light, and harvest, marking the Sun's journey into Capricorn (Makar), signifying longer days and prosperity; their events, like the "Health for Humanity" Yogathon, focus on cultural values, community bonding, and spiritual growth through activities like yoga, suryanamaskar, sharing til-gud, and teaching Hindu culture to children.
